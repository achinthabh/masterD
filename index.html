About MasterD

MasterD is a groundbreaking Sri Lankan rapper who has revolutionized the local hip-hop scene with his unique blend of Sinhala lyrics and international rap styles. Born in Colombo, he began his musical journey at a young age, drawing inspiration from both global hip-hop icons and Sri Lankan musical traditions.

With multiple hit singles and collaborations with international artists, MasterD has established himself as a force to be reckoned with in the Asian hip-hop community. His lyrics often address social issues, personal struggles, and the realities of urban life in Sri Lanka.
